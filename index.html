Journal Prompts

"Rythmic adaptation - not balance - is the key to wellness." -Jennifer Racioppi

Here are some journal prompts which help me reflect and adapt to my shifting energy as the moon travels through the sky, and my interpretation of the key themes of each sign and what it's encouraging me to do. I tend to try and think about the themes of each sign as the moon travels through it, the relationship between the current sun and lunar signs (tension? contradiction? affinity?) and choose 1-2 of these prompts to journal while the moon is in that sign.

According to astrology, you feel a bit more emotionally "at home" when the moon is back in the sign it was when you were born. You might also feel a bit more comfortable when the moon is in your sun sign. The key to astrology is to take what serves you and leave what doesn't.

The name of this web page draws inspiration from the SASS Witches community on reddit.

♈ Aries
actions, beginnings

What obstacles are preventing me from just acting?

Do I feel energetic? What am I doing to energize myself? What actions am I taking towards my goals? Am I overthinking things?

Just do it

♉ Taurus
grounded, secure

What grounds me?

Do I feel secure? Am I committed to my goals? Name one beautiful thing, one tasty and nutritious meal, one good song, one physical pleasure.

Get grounded, stay committed, and indulge your senses.

♊ Gemini
communication, ideas

Am I communicating well? Who am I feeling connected to? How am I fostering connection? What ideas am I thinking about, and how am I expressing them?

Enable collaborative leadership.

♋ Cancer
nurturing

How am I nurturing my own self? How am I nurturing others? What and who makes me feel cared for? How am I caring for others? Am I safe, comfortable, and secure? Check in with myself - how am I feeling?

Do things to help build your self-esteem.

♌ Leo
fun, pride

3 reasons I'm proud of myself.

What is going well for me? What are my strengths? What are my fears, and how am I addressing them? What are the needs of my inner child? How will I have fun today? What are my best qualities? What are my successes?

Celebrate yourself, your accomplishments, and strengths.

♍ Virgo
service, refinement

What is my routine? How can I refine my routine? What habits make me feel strong? What are my responsibilities? What can I improve?
